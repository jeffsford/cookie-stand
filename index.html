WELCOME

Welcome to the online home of Seattle's legendary Pat's Salmon Cookies. Pat's currently have five locations in the Seattle area, and we are rapidly expanding. While you here, be sure to check on upcoming events in the area and check out the Pat's merchandise that is available for sale.

About Us

Pat's Salmon cookies was founded in 2016 when our founder was tinkering with his famous cookie recipe on a cold and gray Seattle day. The result was something distinctly Seattle: salmon shaped cookies that are actually made with real salmon!
